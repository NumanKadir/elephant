🍽️ What Do Elephants Eat?

Elephants are herbivores, meaning they eat only plants! They can eat for up to 16 hours every day.

🌿 Favorite Foods

  • Grass
  • Leaves
  • Fruits (like bananas and mangoes)
  • Bark
  • Roots

Learn more about elephant food »

🕒 How Often Do They Eat?

Elephants eat almost all day and night. They use their trunks to grab leaves, pull down branches, or dig up roots.

⚖️ How Much Do They Eat?

  • 300 pounds (135 kg) of food a day
  • 50 gallons (190 liters) of water daily

That’s about the size of a big bathtub full of water!
