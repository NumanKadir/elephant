Before modern elephants, there were woolly mammoths — their ancient, furry cousins! Mammoths lived during the Ice Age and had long, shaggy hair and giant curved tusks to help them survive the cold. Even though they looked a bit different, they were part of the same elephant family. Scientists believe that some mammoths were as tall as 4 meters and weighed more than a school bus!
