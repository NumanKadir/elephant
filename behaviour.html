🍽️ How Do Elephants Find Food?

Elephants love to eat leaves, grass, fruit, and bark!

  • They use their trunks to grab food from trees or the ground
  • They eat for many hours every day—up to 300 kg!

🛡️ How Do Elephants Stay Safe?

  • Elephants live in big family groups called herds
  • They protect each other, especially the babies
  • Adults use their size and tusks to scare away predators

😴 Sleeping Habits

  • Elephants sleep only 2–4 hours per night
  • They sometimes sleep standing up
  • In safe places, they lie down to nap
