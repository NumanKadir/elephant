🌍 Where on Earth Are Elephants Found?

Elephants live in Africa and Asia.

  • African Elephants live in savannas, deserts, and forests.
  • Asian Elephants live in tropical rainforests and grasslands.

🍃 What Natural Surroundings Do Elephants Need?

Elephants need:

  • Plenty of water to drink and cool off in
  • Trees for shade and food (leaves and bark)
  • Open space to walk and play — they walk many miles a day!

🏠 Where Do Elephants Make Their Home?

Elephants don’t build homes like people. They live:

  • Under trees for shade
  • Near rivers or waterholes
  • In family groups called herds
