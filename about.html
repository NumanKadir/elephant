🐘 All About Elephants

🌍 Where Do Elephants Live?

Elephants live in many places across Africa and Asia. They love to stay near water, trees, and open land.

👪 Elephant Families

Elephants live in groups called herds. A herd is usually led by the oldest female, called the matriarch. Baby elephants are called calves, and everyone in the herd helps take care of them!

📏 How Big Are Elephants?

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth! Some can grow up to 13 feet (4 meters) tall and weigh over 14,000 pounds (6,350 kg)!

🍽️ What Do Elephants Eat?

Elephants are herbivores, which means they only eat plants! They spend a lot of time eating—sometimes up to 16 hours every day!

🌿 Favorite Foods

Elephants love to munch on:

  • Grass
  • Leaves
  • Fruits
  • Bark
  • Roots

Their favorite fruits include bananas, mangoes, and wild berries.

🕒 How Often Do Elephants Eat?

Elephants eat every day, and they don’t have set meal times. They eat almost all the time, using their trunks to grab food or dig for roots.

⚖️ How Much Do They Eat?

An adult elephant can eat up to:

  • 300 pounds (135 kg) of food each day
  • Drink up to 50 gallons (190 liters) of water

That’s a lot—almost as much as a big bathtub of water!
