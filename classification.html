🔬 Elephant Scientific Name & Classification

🧪 Scientific Name

Elephants belong to the family Elephantidae. There are two main species of elephants:

  • African Elephant – Loxodonta africana
  • Asian Elephant – Elephas maximus

🧬 Subspecies

🌍 African Elephant (Loxodonta)

  • Savanna Elephant – Loxodonta africana
  • Forest Elephant – Loxodonta cyclotis

🌏 Asian Elephant (Elephas maximus)

  • Indian Elephant – Elephas maximus indicus
  • Sumatran Elephant – Elephas maximus sumatranus
  • Sri Lankan Elephant – Elephas maximus maximus
  • Bornean Elephant – Elephas maximus borneensis
