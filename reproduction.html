🐘 Elephant Mating and Reproduction

🤰 What Is the Gestational Period?

Elephants have the longest pregnancy of any land animal! A baby elephant grows in its mother’s belly for about 22 months. That’s almost 2 years!

👶 How Many Babies at a Time?

Most of the time, an elephant has one baby at a time. It’s very rare, but sometimes they have twins.

👩‍👧 How Long Do Babies Stay With Their Family?

Baby elephants, called calves, stay with their mothers and herd for a long time—usually 8 to 10 years! During that time, they learn everything they need to survive.
