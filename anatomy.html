📏 Size and Weight

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth!

  • African Elephants: Up to 13 feet tall and 7,500 kg
  • Asian Elephants: Up to 10 feet tall and 5,500 kg

🧠 Special Body Parts

  • Trunk: Used to eat, drink, smell, and play!
  • Tusks: Big teeth for digging and fighting
  • Ears: Help cool down like a fan

🌬️ Breathing and Life

  • Elephants breathe through their trunks
  • They live up to 60–70 years
