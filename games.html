🧠 Fun Elephant Questions!

1️⃣ What do elephants use their trunks for? 🐘👃🍴

To eat, drink, and smell! 🥤🍃👃

2️⃣ How much can an elephant eat in a day? 🍽️

About 300 pounds (135 kg) of food! That’s a LOT of salad! 🥗🐘

3️⃣ Do elephants live alone or in groups? 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

They live in families called herds! ❤️👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

4️⃣ Can elephants swim? 🌊🐘

Yes! Elephants are great swimmers and use their trunks like snorkels! 🏊‍♂️🐘

5️⃣ What sound does an elephant make? 🔊

They trumpet! 🎺🐘 It’s loud and proud!
